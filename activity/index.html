    Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butler
  • 2tbs cream

    Method

  1. Melt the butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. One butter has melted add a cream and eggs
  4. Using the spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
